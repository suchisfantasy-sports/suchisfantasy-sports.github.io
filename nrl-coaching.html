// FAQ

What platform does the coaching cover? ›
SuperCoach and DraftStars are the primary focus. If you play another format, mention it in the intake form and we'll do our best to adapt.
When does the weekly coaching cycle run? ›
You submit your squad Monday evening. We review and send your brief by Tuesday morning. Final check-in Wednesday before lockout.
Can I cancel the weekly plan? ›
Yes — after the minimum 4-week commitment, you can stop at any time. Just let your coach know before the next billing cycle.
What's included in "direct coach access"? ›
You communicate via the weekly check-in form. Season clients get priority responses, typically same-day. Weekly clients receive a brief within 24 hours of submission.
Is this worth it if I'm already active in the Discord? ›
The Discord gives you community intelligence. Coaching gives you personal advice on YOUR team — your specific trades, your specific squad, your specific opponent. It's a different product.
What if I join mid-season? ›
You can start any time. Season pricing stays at $200 regardless of when you join. Weekly plan starts from your first payment week.
