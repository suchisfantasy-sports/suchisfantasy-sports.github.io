# Such Is Fantasy — SEO Guidelines

## Site Focus
Fantasy sports intelligence for Australian players. Primary sports: NRL (SuperCoach, DraftStars) and NBA. Secondary: EPL (FPL) and NFL.

## Primary Keyword Clusters

### NRL Fantasy (highest priority)
- SuperCoach tips [round X]
- NRL fantasy trade targets
- SuperCoach captain picks
- NRL late mail / team news
- SuperCoach buy sell hold
- NRL DraftStars tips
- SuperCoach breakeven calculator
- NRL fantasy price changes

### NBA Fantasy
- NBA fantasy tips
- NBA DraftKings tips Australia
- NBA fantasy trade targets
- NBA fantasy waiver wire picks

### Fantasy Tools
- SuperCoach trade calculator
- Fantasy breakeven calculator
- DraftStars lineup optimizer
- Fantasy bye planner NRL

### Community / Brand
- Such Is Fantasy
- fantasy sports Discord Australia
- NRL fantasy community

## Content Length Requirements
- **Weekly tips/picks:** 800-1,500 words (time-sensitive, publish before lockout)
- **Guides & strategy:** 1,500-2,500 words
- **Tool pages:** 500-800 words + the tool itself
- **Pillar content:** 2,500-4,000 words max
- Round-specific content has a short shelf life — optimise for speed over depth

## On-Page SEO Rules
- **Title tag:** Include round number + sport + year where relevant (e.g., "SuperCoach Tips Round 8 2026")
- **Meta description:** Action-oriented, under 155 chars, include primary keyword
- **H1:** One per page, includes primary keyword
- **URL structure:** Short, lowercase, hyphenated (e.g., `/supercoach-tips-round-8`)
- **Images:** Alt text with keyword context, compressed, WebP

## Internal Linking
- Every article links to relevant tools (calculators, planners)
- Round tips link to previous round's tips and captain picks
- All content links to membership/pricing page
- See `internal-links-map.md` for the full linking strategy

## Seasonal Content Strategy
- **NRL Season (Mar-Oct):** Peak content — weekly tips, trades, captain picks, DraftStars
- **NBA Season (Oct-Apr):** Secondary content — weekly tips, waiver wire, DraftKings
- **EPL Season (Aug-May):** FPL gameweek tips
- **NFL Season (Sep-Feb):** Weekly tips, Best Ball
- **Off-season:** Draft guides, preseason rankings, tool updates, strategy evergreen content
