# Such Is Fantasy — CRO Best Practices

## Primary Conversion Goal
**Fantasy Edge subscription ($9.99/mo)** — this is the volume play.

## Secondary Goals
1. Free Discord signup (top of funnel)
2. Fantasy Lab upgrade ($24.99/mo)
3. Tool usage (engagement → conversion)
4. Email list / content consumption

## Conversion Funnel
```
Free Discord → Use Free Tools → Read Weekly Content → Hit Paywall → Fantasy Edge ($9.99) → Fantasy Lab ($24.99)
```

---

## Pricing Psychology
- **Anchor:** "Less than a coffee a week" — reframe $9.99/mo as trivial
- **Urgency:** "Founding member pricing — locked in for life"
- **Social proof:** 5K+ members, 73% captain accuracy, 4 sports
- **Risk reversal:** 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid tiers
- **Tier contrast:** Free tier is generous enough to hook, Edge is the sweet spot, Lab is aspirational

## Above-the-Fold Rules
Visitors should understand within 5 seconds:
1. **What** — Fantasy sports tips, tools, and community
2. **Who** — For competitive fantasy players (NRL, NBA, EPL, NFL)
3. **Why** — Better intel than your rivals
4. **What next** — Join for $9.99/mo or try free Discord

## CTA Hierarchy
1. **Primary (cyan solid):** "Join the Community" / "Get Fantasy Edge"
2. **Secondary (ghost outline):** "See Plans" / "Try Free"
3. **Tertiary (text link):** "Join Discord Free" in footer

## Trust Signals to Always Include
- 5K+ community members
- 73% captain pick accuracy
- 52 weeks of content per year
- 4 sports covered
- Money-back guarantee
- "Founding member" exclusivity

## Page-Specific CRO

### Homepage
- Hero drives to Edge signup or free Discord
- Three pillars build value before pricing section
- Content calendar shows volume of what they'd get
- Pricing section uses most-popular badge on Edge tier

### Tool Pages
- Free tools available to everyone (lead gen)
- Advanced tools gated behind Lab membership
- Each tool page has "Upgrade to unlock" CTA

### Blog / Tips Content
- Weekly tips are partially free, full analysis behind paywall
- Every article ends with membership CTA
- Sidebar or sticky CTA on long-form content

## Key Metrics to Track
- Free Discord → Edge conversion rate
- Edge → Lab upgrade rate
- Tool page → signup conversion
- Time on site before signup
- Content piece that drives most conversions
