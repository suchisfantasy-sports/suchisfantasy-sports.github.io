# Such Is Fantasy — Brand Voice

## Who We Are
Such Is Fantasy is a multi-sport fantasy sports intelligence platform for Australian men (30-50) who play fantasy NRL and NBA. We provide expert tips, real-time injury alerts, trade tools, and a Discord community of 5K+ members covering NRL, EPL, NBA, and NFL.

## Tagline
Expert Fantasy Sports Intelligence

## Tone
- **Confident and direct** — we know our stuff and don't hedge
- **Matey but not try-hard** — Australian bloke energy, not corporate sports media
- **Data-backed** — stats, percentages, and specifics over vague hype
- **Competitive edge** — everything frames the reader as smarter than their league rivals
- **Never condescending** — assume the audience knows fantasy basics

## Voice Examples
- Yes: "Stop losing to people with better intel"
- Yes: "Expert picks. Before lockout."
- Yes: "Late mail. First."
- No: "We're passionate about helping you on your fantasy journey"
- No: "Our innovative platform leverages cutting-edge analytics"
- No: "Whether you're a beginner or advanced player..."

## Audience
- **Primary:** Australian men, 30-50, playing fantasy NRL (SuperCoach, DraftStars) and NBA
- **Secondary:** EPL (FPL) and NFL fantasy players
- **Mindset:** Competitive, time-poor, want an edge without doing hours of research themselves
- **Platforms they're on:** Discord, TikTok, YouTube
