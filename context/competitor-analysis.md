# Such Is Fantasy — Competitor Analysis

## Market Position
Multi-sport fantasy intelligence platform. Differentiation: tools + community + real-time alerts in one subscription, not just articles.

---

## Primary Competitors (NRL Fantasy)

### SC Playbook
**Website**: scplaybook.com.au
**Offering**: SuperCoach tips, analysis, podcast
**Audience**: SuperCoach players
**Content**: Weekly articles, rankings, podcast
**Gap**: No live tools, no Discord community, no multi-sport coverage

### SuperCoach Traders
**Website**: Likely Facebook/social focused
**Offering**: SuperCoach trade advice, community
**Gap**: Less structured content, no paid tools

### The Keeper League
**Website**: thekeeperleague.com.au
**Offering**: NRL SuperCoach podcast and tips
**Gap**: Podcast-first, less written content, no tools

## Primary Competitors (NBA Fantasy)

### Hashtag Basketball
**Website**: hashtagbasketball.com
**Offering**: NBA fantasy rankings, projections, tools
**Gap**: US-focused, no Australian community, no NRL crossover

### RotoBaller / FantasyPros
**Offering**: Multi-sport fantasy advice
**Gap**: US-centric, no NRL, no Australian timezone consideration

---

## Competitive Advantages (Such Is Fantasy)
- **Multi-sport in one place** — NRL + NBA + EPL + NFL under one roof
- **Australian-first** — timezone-aware alerts, local sport priority
- **Tools + content + community** — not just articles, actual calculators and optimizers
- **Real-time alerts via Discord** — RSS-powered injury/late mail before lockout
- **Founding member pricing** — early adopter lock-in ($9.99/mo, $24.99/mo)
- **73% captain pick accuracy** — provable track record
- **5K+ Discord community** — social proof and engagement

## Content Gaps to Exploit
- NRL DraftStars content is underserved (most focus on SuperCoach only)
- NBA fantasy content for Australians barely exists
- Fantasy tool SEO (calculators, planners) has low competition in AU
- Video content (TikTok/YouTube) for NRL fantasy is growing fast
